About Us

We are a website that should unite gamers. We provide insight about games that we've played, and games that you should pick up. We look to promote games that are perhaps not as well known, or continue promoting games we think deserive it.

This website is nonprofit as we simply just love gaming. Gaming is something that can unite all types of people. We hope that people can always look at us with a good eye, and can rely on us to update them with updated gaming news.
